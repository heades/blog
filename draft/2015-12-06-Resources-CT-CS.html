Books

    • Introductory Texts

      • Category Theory by Steve Awodey
      • An Introduction to Category Theory by Harold Simmons
      • Category Theory for Scientists by David Spivak
      • Categories and Computer Science by R. F. C. Walters
      • Basic Category Theory for Computer Scientists by Benjamin C. Pierce
        • Older Online Version: A Taste of Category Theory for Computer Scientists by Benjamin C. Pierce

    • Categorical Logic

      • Introduction to Higher-Order Categorical Logic by J. Lambek and P. J. Scott
      • Categorical Logic by Andrew Pitts
      • Categories for Types by Roy L. Crole
      • Categorical Logic and Type Theory by Bart Jacobs
      • Categorical Semantics of Linear Logic For All by Valeria de Paiva
      • Categorical Semantics of Linear Logic by Paul-André Melliès
      • Homotopy Type Theory: Univalent Foundations of Mathematics by The Univalent Foundations Program at the Institute for Advanced Study

    • Formal Systems

      • Introduction to Coalgebra: Towards Mathematics of States and Observations by Bart Jacobs

    • Lecture Notes

      • Categories and Computability by J.R.B. Cockett

    • Reference Texts

      • Category Theory for the Working Mathematician by Saunders Mac Lane
      • Toposes, Triples and Theories by Michael Barr and Charles Wells
